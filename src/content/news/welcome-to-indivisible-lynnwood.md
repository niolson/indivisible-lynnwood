We're excited to launch the new Indivisible Lynnwood website! This is your hub for staying connected with our local grassroots community.

## Who We Are

Indivisible Lynnwood is a local chapter of the national Indivisible movement. We are a group of dedicated community members working together to protect democratic values, advocate for progressive policies, and hold our elected officials accountable.

## What You'll Find Here

- **News & Updates** — Stay informed about local actions, events, and advocacy opportunities.
- **Events** — Find upcoming meetings, canvassing sessions, phone banks, and community gatherings.
- **Get Involved** — Ready to make a difference? Join us and find out how you can contribute.

## Get Connected

The best way to stay involved is to [join our group](/join). We'll keep you updated on upcoming events, action alerts, and ways to make your voice heard.

Together, we are indivisible.
